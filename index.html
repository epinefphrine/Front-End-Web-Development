Louisiana: A state, under God, united in purpose and ideals, confident that justice shall prevail for all of those abiding here.

Wherever you are in the state, you can experience the rich culture, amazing food, and beautiful nature.

Founded on April 30th, 1812, Louisiana was a prime spot for settlements to due its water access and flat land that made it easier for travel.

The state of Louisiana known for its distincitve "boot" shape, leading many residents to call it "The Boot."

The Pelican State

Louisiana is commonly known as the Pelican State due to the large population of brown pelicans along its coastlines. Nestled in the Gulf of Mexico, Louisiana's marshy waterways offer a fantastic host for pelicans. Louisiana officially adopted the pelican as its state bird in 1966.
